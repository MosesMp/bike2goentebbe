1. What is Bike2Go Entebbe?
Bike2Go Entebbe is a guided cycling tour and bike rental business located in Entebbe, Uganda. We offer a unique way to explore the beautiful city of Entebbe and its surroundings through our guided tours and bike rentals.
2. What kind of bikes do you have for rent?
We have a wide range of bikes for rent, including road bikes, hybrid bikes, and mountain bikes. All of our bikes are well-maintained and equipped with comfortable seats, water bottle holders, and other necessary accessories.
3. Can I rent a bike for the whole day?
Yes, you can rent a bike for the whole day or for as many days as you need. We offer flexible rental packages to meet your individual needs.
4. What is included in the guided tours?
Our guided tours include a knowledgeable and experienced tour guide, a well-maintained bike, a helmet, water, and snacks. The tours are designed to show you the best of Entebbe and its surroundings, including local landmarks, historical sites, and hidden gems.
5. How long are the guided tours?
The length of our guided tours varies, depending on the tour you choose. Some tours are half-day tours, while others are full-day tours. You can choose a tour that fits your schedule and interests.
6. Can I customize my own tour?
Yes, we can help you design a custom tour that fits your individual needs and interests. Whether you want to explore specific areas of Entebbe or create your own itinerary, we can work with you to create a tour that is tailored to your needs.
7. Are there age restrictions for the tours and bike rentals?
Yes, you must be at least 16 years old to rent a bike and participate in our guided tours. Children under the age of 16 can participate in our tours, but must be accompanied by an adult.
8. Do I need to have previous cycling experience to participate in the tours and rent a bike?
No, you do not need to have previous cycling experience to participate in our tours or rent a bike. Our bikes are designed to be easy to use and our tour guides are knowledgeable and experienced, so they can help you get the most out of your experience.
9.What is the cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy is as follows: if you cancel more than 72 hours before the tour or rental, you will receive a full refund. If you cancel less than 72 hours before the tour or rental, you will not receive a refund. We understand that sometimes life can be unpredictable, so please let us know as soon as possible if you need to cancel your tour or rental.
10.How can I book a tour or rent a bike?
You can book a tour or rent a bike by visiting our website, or by contacting us directly. Our booking process is simple and easy, and our friendly staff are always available to help you with any questions you may have.
