About Us

Bike2go Entebbe Bicycle Tour Company is a 5-year-old small business that offers guided bike tours and bike rentals in Entebbe, Uganda. Founded by two brothers who are from Entebbe, they noticed that many visitors to the town often miss out on its beauty due to its reputation as simply a stopover before heading to the city. The brothers recognized the need to showcase the unique beauty of Entebbe and provide an alternative, sustainable means of exploring the town. They believe that by promoting bike travel, they can not only provide a fun and exciting way to experience Entebbe but also contribute to a more environmentally-friendly tourism industry. With Bike2go, visitors can discover the hidden gems of Entebbe and enjoy the town in an authentic and eco-friendly way. We are proud to have had more than 105 people recommend us recently, and we are constantly working to improve our services to provide an even better experience for our customers.

Our Trips

Short Tour 2.5-3hrs 30$ Ppn

For those with limited time, we offer a 2.5-3 hour bike tour of Entebbe for $30. This tour takes you through the main and minor places in the municipality, allowing you to see the highlights of Entebbe. It's a great option for those who want to make the most of their visit and it's a great value for the cost.

Layover Tour 3.5hrs 35$,4hrs 45$ Ppn

Bike2go Entebbe Layover Tour offers 2 options: 4 hour tour for $45 (includes Reptile Village, Botanical Gardens, market, fishing landing site, beach and other main places) or 3.5 hour tour for $35 (same places, no Reptile Village). Both tours showcase Entebbe's main sights and offer birdwatching at the Botanical Gardens

History & Cultural Tour 40$ Ppn

Our Bike2Go Entebbe tour is a unique way to experience the city's history and culture while biking. Led by an expert guide, you'll visit the Entebbe Botanical Gardens, the Entebbe Zoo, the Uganda Museum and Ndere Cultural Centre. The tour includes bike rental, guide and entrance fee for 40$

Bike and Boat 75$ Ppn,50$ Ppn Group Version

we offer the Entebbe-Bussi Island Bike & Boat that combines biking with a boat ride to Bussi Island on Lake Victoria, known for its beautiful beaches and lush vegetation. The tour includes an optional zip line and obstacle course at the end. Costs $75 for one person, and $50 per person for groups of 2 or more

Bicycle Hire

Rent a bike and explore Entebbe on your own! Our well-maintained bikes can be rented for $25 for one day, or $20 per day if you rent for three or more days. Each rental comes with a lock, helmet, and map of the area, so you can have a worry-free adventure. Whether you want to bike along the beautiful shores of Lake Victoria or explore the vibrant local markets, we have the perfect bike for you at an affordable price.
